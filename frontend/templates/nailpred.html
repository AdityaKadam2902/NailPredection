Guidance

For best results.

Natural lighting

Use daylight or bright, even artificial light. Avoid harsh flash directly on the nail — it washes out surface detail the model relies on.

Fill the frame

The nail should occupy most of the frame in sharp focus. Keep the camera steady and close enough that nail edges are clearly defined.

No polish or acrylics

Remove all nail polish, gel, acrylics, or overlays before photographing. Coatings obscure the nail plate features used for classification.

Clean, dry nails

Wash hands before photographing. Lotion, dirt, or moisture can alter color values and degrade model accuracy.
